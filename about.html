Becky Peltz: Full Stack Developer

I am a full stack developer with an interest in Data Visualization. I like the fact that frameworks are moving us in the direction of building component trees and mapping the data hierarchically through the components. Using key/value unstructured data stores is appealing from the perspective of making your data match your view, but I enjoy modeling and implementing a SQL database when I want to run transactional CRUD. I appreciate a well thought out authentication sequence, as it is critical to a site that is managing users and wants to keep them. I'm an experienced mentor and look for the challenge of introducting new technology to a system.
